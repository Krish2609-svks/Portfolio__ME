# CMS User Guide

Welcome to the Mech Portfolio CMS. This guide explains how to manage your content.

## Logging In
1. Navigate to the CMS URL (e.g., `https://cms.nambikrishnan.com`).
2. Enter your email and password.
3. Upon success, you will be redirected to the Dashboard.

## Dashboard Overview
The dashboard provides a high-level overview of your portfolio.
You can view recent messages from the Contact form and quick stats regarding your content.

## Managing Singletons (Settings)
Singletons are global settings that affect the whole site.
- **Hero**: Edit the main title, subtitle, description, and upload a resume or hero image.
- **About**: Edit your biography and the image displayed in the About section.
- **Contact Info**: Update your Email, LinkedIn, GitHub, Location, and Availability status.
- **SEO Settings**: Define global meta tags (Title, Description, Keywords) to improve search engine rankings.

## Managing Lists
Lists are collections of items (e.g., Skills, Experience).
1. Navigate to the desired section (e.g., `Skills`) via the sidebar.
2. Click **Create New**.
3. Fill out the form. 
4. **Drafts vs Published**: Note the `Published` toggle. If disabled, the item is saved as a Draft and will NOT appear on the public portfolio.
5. Click **Save**.

## Inbox
The Inbox stores all messages received from the Contact Form on your portfolio.
- **Search**: Find messages by Name, Email, or Subject.
- **Filters**: View Unread, Read, or Archived messages.
- **Export**: Click the "Export CSV" button to download all visible messages to a spreadsheet.

## Media Library
The Media Library manages all images and files (like your Resume PDF).
- Ensure files are optimized before uploading (though the public site optimizes images automatically).
- You can copy the URL of an uploaded file to use in Markdown text areas.
